The Format System

Five Lenses, One Topic

Understanding improves when a topic is examined from multiple angles. Each WhyItTV topic is explored through five distinct formats—watch one, or go deeper with all five.

01

Explainer

The narrative foundation. Where did this come from? What did it accomplish? The chronological story with meaning.

02

Debate

Competing interpretations without declaring a winner. Multiple reasonable lenses, ending with better questions.

03

Critique

Decisions, constraints, and consequences. What made sense then, what happened when it hit reality.

04

Deep Dive

Systems under the story. The mechanisms, structures, incentives, and logistics that shaped outcomes.

05

Infographic

Visual compression of the story and structure. Key facts, timelines, and relationships at a glance.
